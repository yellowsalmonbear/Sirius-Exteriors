The Process

Once you have received and accepted a quote for the project, project planning and preparation will begin. As the project commences, our goal is to identify and communicate any potential challenges or surprises as they occur with the homeowner in order to provide realistic expectations and timelines of the work needed in order for the project to successfully reach completion.

While the size and general scope of the work involved in any project is important to consider through this process, the terrain, height, and materials used will impact your final quote and overall installation time.
