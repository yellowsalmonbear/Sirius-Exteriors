BCWITT Intro to Trades Kids Camp

In the Summer of 2022 I had the opportunity to facilitate an introductory trades workshop at the Discover Trades Day Camp. This camp was a partnership between BC Women In The Trades, the BC Trades Women Society and the Industry Training Authory of BC. Together we were able to make projects such as concrete planters, extension cords, water guns and through a variety of fun trades-related projects for young apprentices in training.
