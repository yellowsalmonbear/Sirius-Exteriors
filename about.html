About Sirius Exteriors

At Sirius Exteriours, we take pride in the quality of our work and do our best to ensure customer satisfaction on every job big or small. We strive to add value and beauty to all the homes and projects we work on by ensuring that our work is done with the high quality and care homeowners can expect. We put our best effort into every project so that we can produce seriously superior exteriors with low future maintenance costs. Here at Sirius Exteriors, our commitment to the community is to provide all of our past, present and future clients with the best products, safe work practices, and customer service that they deserve.

About Meghann B.

Carpenter Meghann B. is a red seal carpenter with over 15 years experience, and has been an active resident in the Prince Rupert community since 2016. She is a practicing carpenter who specializes in exteriors, residential, industrial and commercial construction. She also has a special interest for historical restorations, wood art, and timberframing. As a neurodivergent woman in the trades, Meghann B has found a passion in empowering others in her local community to learn hands-on skills through sharing her knowledge of a career in the trades. Meghann B is a Representative of BCCWITT BC Center for Women In The Trades, and has compleated their Leadership Training in 2020 and 2021 and has strived to be a positive role model for others entering the trades. She beleives that by creating a culture of diversity, equity, and inclusion for all tradeswomen, underrepresented groups, and neurodivergents in British Columbia, others can discover their own life long love of the trades as she has.
